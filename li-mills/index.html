01 — The Subject

Who Is
Li Mills

Li Mills is a enigma wrapped in a soft, green lima bean shell. He is perhaps most famous for the "fumble of the century" — going after the same girl as AyAron and somehow managing to lose even when AyAron also fumbled. He is known for a specific type of "lowkey pred" energy, being a sophomore who decided a freshman was his primary target. Above all, Li Mills is the king of the non-sequitur; he will interrupt a serious debate about the economy to tell you about a cloud that looked like a shoe.

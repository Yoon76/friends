01 — The Dual Identity

Who Is
Sam Bradih

By day, Sam Bradley is the quintessential 5'11" nonchalant dreadhead who says absolutely nothing in class. He is a ghost in the hallway, silent and unbothered. But when the sun goes down, he changes into a man you would never know. A man of specific values, Sam has made his stance clear: snow bunnies are not his type. He only values the true black queen. He is Li Mills' homie for life, often found assisting in various shenanigans—though he is just as likely to leave Li in the dust if things get too loud.

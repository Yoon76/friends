Who Is
Preston

Preston is the living embodiment of the Little Prince from Clash Royale — small, royal, and surprisingly dangerous if you let him set up. While others are worrying about high school, Preston is already manifesting his future as the undisputed leader of an ASU fraternity. He is known for his ability to "mog clavicular," a feat of physical dominance that leaves observers confused but impressed. When he's not preparing for his royal frat future, he's in the water, where he moves with a hydro-dynamic efficiency that suggests he might actually be part fish.
